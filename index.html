The Problem That Started It All

The day of region finals, I tore every ligament in my knee. My quadriceps fired but my knee didn't respond. The signal was gone. I became obsessed: Why didn't my body warn me?

Most injury prevention devices detect only muscle fatigue. They miss ACL, meniscus, patellar strap, and bone stress risks. This system uses eight sensors to monitor multiple tissues simultaneously.

ACL Risk & Patellar Strap

High force + near extension + valgus angle. Calculated from dual IMUs and force sensors.

Meniscus Risk

Twisting under load + impact force. Detected via gyroscope and force sensors.

Bone Stress

Cumulative impact force from repeated jumps/landings. Prevents stress fractures.

Muscle Fatigue

EMG amplitude drop over time. Detects when muscles can no longer stabilize the joint.

Real Testing, Real Results
